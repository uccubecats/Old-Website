Who We Are

The UC CubeCats is a student organization at the University of Cincinnati dedicated to the education of its members through the development and launch of CubeSats (small, standardized satellites). While we are a predominantly undergraduate engineering organization, we accept university students of every level and every major.



CATiSE Program

The CubeCats Applied Training in Space Exploration (CATiSE) Program is designed to teach new members of the UC CubeCats systems engineering, project leadership skills, and technical skills for space like environments



CubeSat Program

Our CubeSat program is comprised of dedicated members working on a single CubeSat mission, a ground station for communicating with future CubeSat missions, and a Helmholtz cage for testing ADAC systems.
